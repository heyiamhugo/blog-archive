Welcome to Hugo’s Notes!<\/h2>\n\n\n\n

This is my personal blog, where I share my thoughts and experiences on design and other topics. I’m a designer by trade, but I’m also interested in a wide range of other things, including technology, art, music, movies, books and even f1.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

I started this blog as a way to share my knowledge and insights with others, and to learn from others as well. I hope that my writing will be helpful and informative, and that it will inspire others to think critically about the world around them.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

If you’re interested in learning more about design, or if you’re just looking for a different perspective on the world, I encourage you to browse my blog posts. I hope you find something that interests you!<\/p>\n\n\n\n

What to expect on Hugo’s Notes:<\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

    \n
  • Design:<\/strong> I’ll be writing about all aspects of design, from how I run my design studio and how design affects my life and my surroundings to product reviews and tips for designers like me. Everyone loves a good tip!<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n\n\n\n
      \n
    • Other topics:<\/strong> I’m also interested in a wide range of topics. I’ll be writing about these topics from time to time, but my focus will always be on design.<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n\n\n\n

      About me:<\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

      I’m a designer with over half a decade of experience. Although I’m still on the early years of my career, I’ve become extremely versatile thanks to my work with several non-profit organizations. Nowadays I’m working hard on my internship at a large scale multinational company in Portugal and I’m also building from the ground up my own graphic design studio – studio nove.<\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

      In addition to my work as a designer, I’m also a speaker, writer and also part-time youtuber. I love to share my work and my perspective on how design has changed and can change the world so if you want me to speak at your venue please hit me up!<\/p>\n\n\n\n

      I’m always looking for new ways to learn and grow. I’m excited to share my knowledge and insights with others through this blog.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

      Thank you for visiting Hugo’s Notes!<\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n
